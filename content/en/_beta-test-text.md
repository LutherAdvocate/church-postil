## ✝️ Welcome to the Luther Hub Workspace Beta

Welcome to a digital workspace designed specifically for historical theological study and sermon preparation.

While the backend database is undergoing a major restructure to organize Martin Luther's historic _Church Postil_ sermons by liturgical season, the core interactive reading tools are fully operational. We need your real-world feedback to perfect this system for pastors and students worldwide.

## 🛠️ Features Available for Testing Today

- **Interactive Highlighting:** Select and mark critical theological insights directly within Luther's text.
- **Active Note-Taking:** Draft your sermon outlines or study notes right next to the source script.
- **Local Backups:** Save your annotations safely on your device, create secure backups, and restore old sessions instantly upon your next visit.
- **Export Tools:** Print your finalized study notes or email them straight to your desktop for immediate pulpit use.
